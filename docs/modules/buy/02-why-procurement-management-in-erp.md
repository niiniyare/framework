## Why Procurement Management in ERP

### The Procurement Challenge

Without an integrated procurement system, organizations face:

**Process Inefficiencies:**
- Manual purchase requisitions and approvals
- Disconnected supplier communication
- Paper-based purchase orders
- Delayed goods receipt processing
- Invoice matching errors
- Payment delays and disputes

**Cost Overruns:**
- Maverick spending (off-contract purchases)
- Duplicate orders
- Emergency purchases at premium prices
- Poor supplier negotiation leverage
- Inventory stockouts or overstocking
- Untracked supplier performance

**Compliance Risks:**
- Budget overruns
- Unauthorized purchases
- Missing audit trails
- Tax compliance issues
- Contract violations
- Regulatory non-compliance

**Visibility Gaps:**
- Unknown spend patterns
- Supplier performance blind spots
- Budget tracking challenges
- Inventory level uncertainties
- Payment obligation unknowns

### Key ERP Procurement Capabilities

1. **Purchase Requisition Management**
   - Employee self-service portals
   - Automated approval routing
   - Budget validation
   - Catalog-based ordering

2. **Supplier Management**
   - Centralized supplier database
   - Performance tracking
   - Contract management
   - Supplier portals

3. **Purchase Order Processing**
   - Template-based PO creation
   - Multi-currency support
   - Change order management
   - Electronic transmission (EDI/Email)

4. **Goods Receipt**
   - Mobile receipt capture
   - Quality inspection integration
   - Batch/serial number tracking
   - Barcode scanning

5. **Invoice Processing**
   - 3-way matching automation
   - Exception handling
   - Early payment discount capture
   - Automated GL posting

6. **Payment Management**
   - Payment term optimization
   - Cash flow planning
   - Payment batch processing
   - Supplier statement reconciliation

7. **Analytics & Reporting**
   - Spend analysis
   - Supplier scorecards
   - Budget vs. actual reports
   - Price variance analysis
