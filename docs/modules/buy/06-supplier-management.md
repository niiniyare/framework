```markdown

  Definition:
    - Critical to operations
    - High spend volume (> 10M annually)
    - Limited alternatives
    - Long-term partnership

  Management:
    - Dedicated account manager
    - Quarterly business reviews
    - Annual contracts with SLAs
    - Joint improvement initiatives
    - Risk monitoring

    - Ace Steel Suppliers (raw materials)

    - LogiTrans (primary logistics)

  Definition:
    - Proven performance
    - Medium spend (2M - 10M annually)
    - Competitive pricing
    - Good service levels

  Management:
    - Annual performance reviews
    - Standard contracts
    - Regular communication
    - Preferred status for RFQs

  Examples:
    - Kenya Fasteners
    - Office Supplies Co
    - Maintenance Services Ltd

  Count: 15 suppliers (15% of spend)

Approved Suppliers:
  Definition:
    - Qualified and verified
    - Low to medium spend
    - Multiple alternatives available

  Management:
    - Bi-annual reviews
    - Transaction-based
    - Competitive quoting

  Count: 50 suppliers (5% of spend)

  Definition:
    - Newly onboarded (< 6 months)
```
