### RFQ Types

```markdown
RFQ CATEGORIES

1. Standard RFQ:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Regular procurement
Items: Cataloged items, standard specs
Suppliers: Multiple approved suppliers
Timeline: 10-14 days
Example: Raw materials, MRO items

2. Technical RFQ:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Complex technical requirements
Items: Equipment, machinery, custom items
Suppliers: Pre-qualified technical suppliers
Timeline: 30-45 days
Includes: Technical proposals, presentations
Example: CNC machines, automation systems

3. Service RFQ:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Service contracts
Items: Maintenance, consultancy, logistics
Suppliers: Service providers
Timeline: 14-21 days
Includes: Service level agreements, methodology
Example: Annual maintenance, security services

4. Framework Agreement RFQ:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Long-term supply arrangement
Items: Recurring purchases
Duration: Usually 1-3 years
Suppliers: Strategic suppliers
Example: Office supplies, utilities

5. Emergency RFQ:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Urgent requirements
Timeline: 24-48 hours
Suppliers: Limited (2-3)
Process: Simplified, verbal quotes accepted
Example: Breakdown spares, production stoppage

6. Two-Stage RFQ (Expression of Interest + RFQ):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Use: Large, complex procurements
Stage 1: EOI - Supplier qualification
Stage 2: RFQ - Technical and commercial proposals
Timeline: 60-90 days
Example: Large projects, plant equipment
```
