### Supplier Classification

```markdown

Classification Criteria:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Strategic Suppliers:

  Annual Spend: >5M KES
  Criticality: High (limited alternatives)
  
  Benefits:
    • Quarterly business reviews
    • Preferred payment terms
    • Joint development initiatives
    • Early new product involvement
    • Long-term contracts
    
  Current Count: 4 suppliers
  Examples: Ace Steel, ChemSupply, Precision Tech

Preferred Suppliers:
  Performance Score: 80-90/100
  Annual Spend: 1-5M KES
  Criticality: Medium
  
  Benefits:
    • Regular performance reviews
    • Standard payment terms
    • Annual contracts
    • Partnership approach
    
  Current Count: 12 suppliers

Approved Suppliers:
  Performance Score: 70-80/100
  Annual Spend: <1M KES
  Criticality: Low (multiple alternatives)
  
  Benefits:
    • Transactional relationship
    • Standard terms
    • Spot purchases acceptable
    
  Current Count: 28 suppliers

Probation Suppliers:
  Performance Score: <70/100
  Status: Under review
  
  Actions:
    • Performance improvement plan mandatory
    • 90-day review period
    • Alternative sourcing in progress
    • May be terminated
    
  Current Count: 2 suppliers
  Examples: ElectroTech (quality issues), Industrial Tools (pricing)

Movement Between Tiers:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Promotion Process:
  Approved → Preferred:
    - Maintain 80+ score for 2 consecutive quarters
    - Increase spend or expand categories
    - Pass strategic assessment
    
  Preferred → Strategic:
    - Maintain 90+ score for 3 consecutive quarters
    - Critical category or high spend
    - Pass strategic partnership evaluation
    - Board/Director approval

Demotion Process:
  Any Tier → Probation:
    - Score <70 for 1 quarter
    - Critical quality or compliance issue
    - Immediate review triggered
    
  Probation → Termination:
    - No improvement after 90 days
    - Repeat critical issues
    - Failure to meet improvement plan milestones
```

### Performance Review Process


### Supplier Risk Management

```markdown
RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

Risk Categories:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Financial Risk
   Indicators:
     • Late financial statements
     • Payment disputes with other customers
     • Credit rating downgrades
     • Delayed payments to their suppliers
   
   Mitigation:
     • Annual financial review
     • Credit checks
     • Diversify to alternative suppliers
     • Shorter payment terms if high risk

2. Operational Risk
   Indicators:
     • Single source of supply
     • Limited capacity
     • Outdated technology
     • High staff turnover
   
   Mitigation:
     • Develop backup suppliers
     • Capacity audits
     • Support technology upgrades
     • Long-term contracts for stability

3. Quality Risk
   Indicators:
     • Increasing rejection rates
     • Customer complaints
     • Certificate non-compliance
     • Process changes
   
   Mitigation:
     • Enhanced inspection
     • Supplier audits
     • Quality agreements
     • Performance improvement plans

4. Compliance Risk
   Indicators:
     • Regulatory violations
     • Ethical concerns
     • Sustainability issues
     • Documentation gaps
   
   Mitigation:
     • Compliance audits
     • Code of conduct agreement
     • Training and support
     • Termination if serious
```
